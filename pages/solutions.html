Solutions

We can provide a range of tailored solutions and concentrate on the following areas.

CAD Integrations

Is your vendor charging an arm and a leg for a custom integration? Dragging their feet to build what you need? Let our engineers take a look and we'll give you a quote. (Hint: We're most likely faster and cheaper, and we always pick up the phone.)

Services:

  • CAD-to-CAD integrations — interagency incident/unit sharing and de-duplication.
  • CAD-to-Alert/Station systems — trigger tones, lights, bay doors (e.g., PURVIS, Zetron, Westnet).
  • CAD-to-AVL/MDT — Automatic Vehicle Location and mobile terminals; unit status and location sync.
  • CAD-to-Facilities/Access — doors, gates, and building systems (BMS) with audit and interlocks.
  • CAD-to-EOC/Dashboards — city/county situational awareness boards and common operating pictures.
  • CAD-to-Public/Partner Feeds — coarse-grained, rate-limited APIs for vetted partners or community apps.
  • CAD-to-Analytics/Warehouse — ETL for after-action reviews, QA, and performance reporting.
TAK Plugins and Integrations

TAK is a leading platform for real-time situational awareness developed by the U.S. military. We build secure, custom connectors between TAK and external systems (CAD, sensors, AVL, NG9-1-1 data, and more), with attention to authentication, access control, and reliable operation.

Services:

  • Data bridging (CoT/JSON) with filtering and redaction policies.
  • Gateway services for on-prem, cloud, or field-deployable kits.
  • Operational hardening: TLS, cert lifecycle, and key management.
9-1-1 Testing

We execute formal test suites developed by Texas A&M CACN to validate interfaces and workflows. You get reproducible runs, actionable failure reports, and remediation guidance to reach compliance and interoperability goals.

Services:

  • Test planning, harness setup, and environment capture.
  • Automated runs with artifacts: logs, pcap traces, and summaries.
  • Gap analysis and prioritized fixes to pass required cases.
Security Consulting

We provide practical cybersecurity guidance and hands-on training focused on public-safety operations: identity, device trust, and least-privilege access that won’t slow the job down.

Services:

  • PIV/CAC onboarding, certificate management, and MFA patterns.
  • Password policy, role-based access control, and audit readiness.
  • Network segmentation and incident response tabletop exercises.
