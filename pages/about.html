Meet the Founder

I founded Talarium Labs after completing a PhD in Computer Science at the University of Pennsylvania. I also hold an MSc in Computer Science from the University of Oxford and a BS in Mathematics from Florida State University. My research focuses on building systems that are correct, reliable, and durable under stress. Now, Talarium is bringing that same mindset to emergency communication.

I participate actively in NG911 standardization as a member of these groups:

  • NG911 Interoperability Task Force
  • NENA Futurists Committee
  • NENA i3 Architecture Working Group
  • NENA Interface Specification Development Working Group
  • NENA Emergency Incident Data Object Conveyance Working Group
  • APCO Automated Secure Alarm Protocol (ASAP-to-PSAP) Working Group

I live and work in North Florida and spend time in EOCs and PSAPs to understand what works and what breaks in the field. If you would be interested in meeting to discuss your agency's needs and experience, contact us now and let's meet.

— Lawrence Dunn Founder, Talarium Labs
